About Heritage Treasures

Project Overview

Heritage Treasures: An In-Depth Analysis of UNESCO World Heritage Sites

This project explores UNESCO World Heritage Sites around the world using Tableau visualizations and Flask web development. The analysis focuses on cultural, natural, and mixed heritage sites, their geographical distribution, inscription trends, and country-wise heritage patterns. Interactive dashboards help users understand global heritage preservation and historical significance.
